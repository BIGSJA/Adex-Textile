About



.

The word textile is derived from Latin 'textilis', from 'textus, meaning 'woven'; the past participle form of the verb 'texere', meaning “to weave”. Textile is a fabric made from plant or animal fibres. Archaeological evidence indicates that humans began using woven materials in clothing and shelter about 70,000 years ago. In the late 19th century, the textile industry was one of the key components of the Industrial Revolution. It was the first mass-production industry that used machines in the manufacture of cloth.



Textile is the most popular fashion concept that is created by living beings, who have the unique ability to express themselves through the clothing they wear. The clothing can come in a wide variety of forms for all kinds of occasions and can be very useful. Clothing can help make you feel good about yourself, either by enhancing your looks or just helping you to feel comfortable and secure.
